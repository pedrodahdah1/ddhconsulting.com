The Reality

$48 billion in preventable losses.
The root cause is infrastructure.

Healthcare organizations don't lack data — they lack the architecture to operationalize it. Clinical systems capture what was billed. Clearinghouses record what was adjudicated. Accounting platforms reflect what was collected. But in most organizations, these data sources remain siloed — creating blind spots that drive revenue leakage, delayed decision-making, and compliance exposure.

The consequence is measurable: finance teams spend more time assembling information than analyzing it. Audit readiness depends on institutional knowledge rather than repeatable systems. And leadership decisions are informed by lagging, manually compiled reports rather than real-time intelligence.

We build the data architecture, automated pipelines, and reporting infrastructure that unifies these systems into a single source of truth — purpose-built for healthcare financial performance.

FAQ

Frequently asked.

What industries do you work with?
We specialize in healthcare organizations — hospitals, specialty pharmacies, physician groups, behavioral health — and mid-market companies ($20M–$150M+) across any industry where finance and operations teams need better data visibility.
Do you work with our existing systems?
Yes. We integrate with whatever you're already running — Epic, Athena, Cerner, QuickBooks, NetSuite, Sage, specialty billing platforms, and more. We build on top of your systems, not around them.
Can you work remotely?
Most of our work is performed remotely through secure screen sharing, cloud environments, and documented handoffs. Miami-area clients also have the option of in-person sessions.
What if we already have an IT team?
Great — we work alongside your IT department. They manage your infrastructure; we build the data layer on top of it. Everything we deploy is documented so your team can maintain it long-term.
Is this just Power BI dashboards?
Dashboards are the visible layer, but the real value is underneath — the data pipelines, the data model, and the automation that ensures the numbers are accurate, consistent, and always current. We build the full stack, not just the front end.
Do we own the work product?
100%. Every pipeline, dashboard, data model, and line of documentation is yours. No proprietary lock-in, no recurring licenses to us. When the engagement ends, you own everything.
Can your systems help us prepare for audits or bank reporting?
Yes — audit readiness is built into every engagement. We design data models with full traceability so your team can pull audit-ready financial reports, AR reconciliation, revenue recognition documentation (ASC 606), and covenant compliance packages on demand. When your auditors, lenders, or board members ask questions, the answer is already in the system.
How are you different from a typical BI or Power BI consulting firm?
Most BI firms are staffed by data engineers who build technically sound dashboards but don't understand the numbers underneath. We bring finance and accounting expertise — revenue recognition, contractual allowances, denial code classification, payer contract compliance — alongside the technical build. The result is a system that doesn't just display data, but surfaces the financial insights your team actually needs to make decisions.
